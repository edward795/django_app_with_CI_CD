Opportunity for Non-voice Tutor

Company is an online platform that connects tutors, educators, and students globally. We help our students on our platform with their homework and other academic needs. Our tutors are compensated by helping our students in answering their questions in a text-based form.

Responsibilities:

As a Non-voice Tutor, you are answering homework questions, you earn by providing accurate, original solutions. It’s not the typical face-to-face video tutoring that you are familiar with. You will teach by providing text-based answers. At the same time, you can earn up to $16 per question. You can teach at your most convenient time.

Qualifications:

  • At least a college student who has taken most of the major subjects
  • College graduates and licensed professionals are a plus!
  • Subjects/Majors: Finance, Economics, Human Resource Management, Marketing, Business Law, Operations Management,Managerial Accounting, Project Management, Microeconomics, Management, and Macroeconomics.Statistics and Probability, Financial Accounting, Business - Other, Computer Science, Calculus,Nursing, English, Math - Other, Accounting, Chemistry,Psychology,Algebra,Physics,Health Science, Electrical Engineering, Biology, Mechanical Engineering, Organic chemistry,Sociology, Python Programming, Civil Engineering, Java Programming, US History, Philosophy, and journalism.
  • Note:If your subject/major isn’t mentioned in above list, still you can apply just specify your subject/major.
